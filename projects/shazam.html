Overview

Shazam like application developed in Matlab. Uses frequency analysis in the w domain to determine similarity between song listened to and the database. Displays what song the frequency spectrum is most similar to.

Features
  • Determines the song within 5 seconds of listening to it on speaker with 80% accuracy
  • Supports a notch filter that can filter away a sinusoid anywhere on the spectrum
  • Supports a gaussian filter for filtering out white noise applied at all frequencies
Languages Used

Matlab
